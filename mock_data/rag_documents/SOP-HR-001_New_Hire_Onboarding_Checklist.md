# SOP-HR-001 — New Hire Onboarding Checklist

**Document ID:** SOP-HR-001
**Version:** 5.0
**Effective Date:** 2026-05-01
**Owner:** HR Operations Manager — Mary Aisa
**Visibility:** STAFF ONLY

---

## 1. Purpose

Ensure every new Steamships Trading Company employee (full-time, part-time, contract) has a consistent onboarding experience in their first 30 days. Reduces the 80% basic-error rate the Head of Strategy flagged in 2025.

## 2. Pre-start (Day -3 to Day 0)

| Step | Owner | Done when |
|------|-------|-----------|
| Send welcome email with first-day instructions | HR Coordinator | 3 days before start |
| Prepare laptop, access badge, parking pass | IT | 1 day before start |
| Create Odoo user account with correct division access | IT | Day 0 morning |
| Assign onboarding buddy from the same division | Line Manager | Day -3 |
| Book induction sessions (HR + Safety + IT) | HR Coordinator | Day -3 |

## 3. Day 1

- Welcome by Line Manager at 8:30 AM
- Office tour (restrooms, kitchen, fire exits, first-aid station)
- HR induction (90 min): policies, leave, payroll cycle, code of conduct
- IT induction (60 min): laptop setup, Odoo login, email, chat tools
- Safety induction (60 min): emergency procedures, incident reporting
- Lunch with onboarding buddy

## 4. Week 1 — must complete

- Read all division-specific SOPs (Sales reps: SOP-SHIP-001, SOP-SHIP-004; Property: SOP-PROP-002; Hospitality: SOP-HOSP-003)
- Shadow a colleague for at least 4 hours on a real task
- Complete the AI Chatbot orientation (ask the chatbot 3 practice questions)
- Set up 1-on-1s with the 5 key colleagues in their workflow

## 5. Day 30 — probation review

- Self-assessment submitted via Odoo
- Line Manager assessment submitted
- HR review meeting — confirms continued employment or extension decision
- Certificate of Completion added to employee file

## 6. Required documents from new hire

Before Day 1, the new hire must submit:

1. Signed employment contract
2. PNG Tax File Number (TIN) — or application receipt for new residents
3. Bank account details for payroll (local PGK account)
4. Superannuation nomination form (NASFUND)
5. Emergency contact details
6. Right-to-work documentation (PNG citizenship, work permit, or visa)

## 7. AI Chatbot usage during onboarding

All new hires are encouraged to use the Steamships AI Chatbot (in Odoo backend, menu "Ask AI") as their first point of question for any policy or procedure. The chatbot will:

- Search the SOP library for the answer
- Quote the source document name and section
- Say "I do not know, please ask the HR team" if the answer is not in the documents

This is the company's safety net for the 8 out of 10 new hires who would otherwise make basic errors in their first 3 months.
