## 1. Purpose

Define the standard procedure for accepting, confirming, and executing a container booking across the Consort fleet. Ensures every booking has complete documentation before a vessel sails — no exceptions.

## 2. Scope

Applies to all FCL (Full Container Load) and LCL (Less than Container Load) bookings made through the Lae, Port Moresby, Rabaul, Madang, and Wewak offices. JV partner bookings follow the same procedure unless a written override exists.

## 3. Booking workflow

| Step | Action | Owner | Time SLA |
|------|--------|-------|----------|
| 1 | Receive booking request (email, phone, portal) | Sales Coordinator | Same day |
| 2 | Verify client KYC status (must be "Approved" in CRM) | Sales Coordinator | 1 hour |
| 3 | Check vessel schedule on Consort schedule board | Operations | 30 min |
| 4 | Confirm space allocation with vessel master | Operations | 2 hours |
| 5 | Issue Booking Confirmation to client (email + portal) | Sales Coordinator | 1 hour after step 4 |
| 6 | Collect required documents (see SOP-SHIP-004) | Client (with Sales follow-up) | 24 hours |
| 7 | Cut Gate Pass and pass to terminal operations | Operations | 4 hours before gate cut-off |

## 4. Required booking information

The following must be captured BEFORE issuing a booking confirmation:

- Client name and CRM partner ID
- Origin port and destination port
- Container type and quantity (20ft, 40ft, 40ft HC, reefer)
- Commodity description (per customs HS code if available)
- Estimated cargo weight (kg) and volume (m³)
- Required vessel (if client has a preference) or earliest available
- Shipper and consignee full addresses
- Notify party (third party to be alerted on arrival)

## 5. Cut-off times

| Port | Documentation cut-off | Cargo gate cut-off |
|------|----------------------|--------------------|
| Lae | 24 hours before vessel ETD | 12 hours before vessel ETD |
| Port Moresby | 48 hours before vessel ETD | 24 hours before vessel ETD |
| Rabaul | 24 hours before vessel ETD | 12 hours before vessel ETD |
| Madang | 24 hours before vessel ETD | 12 hours before vessel ETD |

Late documentation: bookings auto-flagged as "AT RISK" in Odoo and Operations must escalate to the Shipping Manager before granting a waiver.
