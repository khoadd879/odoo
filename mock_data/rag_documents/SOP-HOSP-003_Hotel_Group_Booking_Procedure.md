# SOP-HOSP-003 — Hotel Group Booking Procedure

**Document ID:** SOP-HOSP-003
**Version:** 1.8
**Effective Date:** 2026-01-15
**Owner:** Hospitality Sales Manager — David Pomat
**Visibility:** STAFF ONLY

---

## 1. Purpose

Define how group bookings (10 room nights or more) are handled at the Steamships Hotel Group properties: Airways Hotel, Gateway Hotel, Highlander Hotel, and Huon Gulf Hotel.

## 2. When this SOP applies

This procedure applies for any of the following:

- 10 or more room nights (across one or multiple properties)
- Conference or meeting room bookings (any size)
- Wedding or event bookings (50+ guests)
- Corporate negotiated rates (annual contracts)

Individual leisure bookings follow the standard reservation system and do NOT need this procedure.

## 3. Group booking workflow

| Step | Action | Owner | SLA |
|------|--------|-------|-----|
| 1 | Receive group enquiry (corporate, travel agent, wedding planner) | Sales Coordinator | Same day |
| 2 | Check availability across requested properties and dates | Revenue Manager | 4 hours |
| 3 | Prepare proposal with room rates, F&B packages, meeting space | Sales Coordinator | 24 hours |
| 4 | Send proposal to client; offer site inspection if needed | Sales Coordinator | Same day as step 3 |
| 5 | Client confirms; sign group contract | Legal + Sales | 7 days |
| 6 | Collect 30% non-refundable deposit | Finance | 14 days before arrival |
| 7 | Final rooming list due | Client | 7 days before arrival |
| 8 | Final payment due | Finance | 3 days before arrival |

## 4. Negotiated corporate rates

For corporate clients committing to 100+ room nights per year:

- Discount off BAR (Best Available Rate): 10-20% depending on volume
- Includes: breakfast, Wi-Fi, gym, airport transfer (where available)
- Excludes: incidentals (minibar, laundry, F&B beyond breakfast)
- Rate review: every 12 months

## 5. Required documents for group booking contract

1. Signed group contract (Hotel Group legal template)
2. Company registration of the booking party
3. Tax invoice and payment instructions
4. Contact details for the event organiser (on-site decision maker)

## 6. Cancellation policy

| Timing | Refund |
|--------|--------|
| More than 60 days before arrival | Full refund minus 10% admin fee |
| 30-60 days before | 50% refund of deposit |
| Less than 30 days | No refund |

Force majeure clause applies (natural disasters, government travel bans).
