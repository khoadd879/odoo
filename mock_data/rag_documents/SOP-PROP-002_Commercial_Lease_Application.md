# SOP-PROP-002 — Commercial Lease Application Procedure

**Document ID:** SOP-PROP-002
**Version:** 2.0
**Effective Date:** 2026-03-01
**Owner:** Property Leasing Manager — Sarah Kewa
**Visibility:** STAFF ONLY

---

## 1. Purpose

Standardise the way commercial lease enquiries are handled, quoted, and approved across Steamships Property's office, retail, and warehouse portfolio in Port Moresby, Lae, and Mount Hagen.

## 2. Application workflow

| Step | Action | Owner | SLA |
|------|--------|-------|-----|
| 1 | Receive enquiry (phone, email, walk-in, web form) | Leasing Admin | Same day |
| 2 | Match enquiry to available units (size, location, use class) | Leasing Manager | 24 hours |
| 3 | Conduct site inspection with prospective tenant | Property Officer | Within 5 working days |
| 4 | Issue Letter of Offer with indicative price | Leasing Manager | 3 working days after inspection |
| 5 | Tenant accepts and signs Letter of Offer | Tenant | Up to 14 days |
| 6 | KYC and credit check (see SOP-FIN-005) | Finance | 5 working days |
| 7 | Execute lease agreement and collect 3 months deposit | Legal | 7 working days |
| 8 | Hand over keys and log in Property register | Property Officer | Lease start date |

## 3. Required information from prospective tenant

- Company name and registration number
- Two years of financial statements (for leases > PGK 50,000 / month)
- Director ID copies and proof of address
- Intended use of the premises (must match zoning)
- Estimated staff numbers (for car park and fit-out planning)
- Preferred lease term (typically 3, 5, or 10 years)

## 4. Pricing rules

- Base rent is per square metre per month, set by the Property Pricing Committee quarterly.
- Service charge (CAM — Common Area Maintenance) is additional, typically PGK 8 — PGK 15 per m² per month.
- Discounts above 5% on base rent require Head of Property approval.
- Free rent periods (fit-out) are capped at 2 months and require CFO sign-off.
