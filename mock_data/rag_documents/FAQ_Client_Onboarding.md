## General questions

### Q1: How do I become a client of Steamships Trading Company?

Visit our website and fill the single onboarding form. Provide your company name, contact person, email, phone, country, industry, and what service you need. You can also attach your registration documents and KYC pack at the same time. A salesperson will contact you within 24 hours.

### Q2: Is there a fee to register as a client?

No. Registration is free. You only pay when you book a service (shipping, property lease, hotel booking).

### Q3: What documents do I need to provide to be onboarded?

You need to provide eight KYC items:

1. IPA Certificate of Incorporation
2. IRC Tax Identification Number (TIN)
3. Bank reference letter
4. Director ID copies
5. PEP screening consent
6. Credit check consent
7. Insurance certificate (where applicable)
8. Signed standard terms

Your salesperson will guide you through each item.

### Q4: How long does onboarding take?

Typically 7 to 14 working days from form submission, depending on how quickly you return the KYC documents and pass our compliance checks.

### Q5: Which industries do you serve?

We serve four main industries:

- Logistics (shipping, port services, tug and tow, warehousing)
- Property (commercial and residential leases)
- Hospitality (hotel accommodation, meetings and events)
- Joint Ventures (part-owned businesses with PNG partners)

## Shipping-specific questions

### Q6: I want to ship a 20ft container from Lae to Port Moresby. What do I do?

1. Make sure you are onboarded (KYC approved) in our system.
2. Contact our shipping sales desk (shipping@steamships.com.pg) or your assigned salesperson.
3. Provide the booking information (commodity, weight, dates).
4. Submit the required shipping documents (Commercial Invoice, Packing List, Export Permit if applicable).
5. We issue a Booking Confirmation and a Gate Pass for the cargo.
6. Cargo is loaded on the next available vessel (transit: 2 days Lae → POM).

### Q7: What is the price for shipping a 20ft container from Lae to Port Moresby?

Pricing depends on your contract, the cargo, and current fuel surcharge. For a firm quote, contact our shipping sales desk (shipping@steamships.com.pg) or your assigned salesperson. They will send you a formal quotation within one working day.

### Q8: Do I need a dangerous goods declaration?

Only if your cargo is classified as dangerous goods under the IMO IMDG Code (explosives, gases, flammable liquids, flammable solids, oxidizers, toxic, radioactive, corrosives, or miscellaneous). When in doubt, contact our Shipping Compliance team.

## Property questions

### Q9: How do I apply for a commercial lease?

Submit an enquiry through the Steamships Property website or contact the Leasing Manager at property@steamships.com.pg. We will arrange a site inspection within 5 working days and issue a Letter of Offer within 3 working days after the inspection.

### Q10: What is the typical lease term?

Standard lease terms are 3, 5, or 10 years. Shorter terms (1-2 years) are available for smaller units but at a higher per-square-metre rate.

## Hospitality questions

### Q11: How do I book a meeting or event at an Airways Hotel?

Contact our Hospitality Sales Manager at hospitality@steamships.com.pg. For groups of 10+ room nights, follow our Group Booking Procedure — your salesperson will provide a proposal within 24 hours.

### Q12: Can I get a corporate rate?

Yes, if your company commits to 100 or more room nights per year. Corporate rates offer 10-20% off the Best Available Rate and include breakfast, Wi-Fi, gym, and (where available) airport transfer.

## Joint Venture questions

### Q13: How do I propose a joint venture with Steamships?

Contact the Head of Strategy and Transformation at strategy@steamships.com.pg. JV proposals are reviewed quarterly by the JV Investment Committee.
