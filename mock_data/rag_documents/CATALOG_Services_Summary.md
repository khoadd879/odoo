## Our four divisions

### 1. Logistics (Consort + Pacific Towing)

We operate one of the largest shipping fleets in Papua New Guinea, with about 20 vessels visiting 17 ports. Our logistics services include:

- **Container shipping** — FCL (Full Container Load) and LCL (Less than Container Load)
- **Port handling / stevedoring** — loading, unloading, and storage
- **Tug and tow services** — Pacific Towing operates tugs in Lae and Port Moresby
- **Warehousing** — secure facilities for LCL consolidation and short-term storage
- **Customs clearance** — full brokerage service through our licensed customs team
- **Project cargo** — heavy lift, out-of-gauge, and breakbulk for mining and infrastructure clients

### 2. Property

We own and lease commercial, retail, warehouse, and residential properties across PNG:

- **Office space** — prime CBD locations in Port Moresby (Stanley House, Government Building)
- **Retail** — ground-floor shopfronts in commercial centres
- **Warehouses** — Lae Industrial Park, Mount Hagen Plaza
- **Residential** — serviced apartments in Boroko (POM) and Lae
- **Property management** — full service for owner-investors

### 3. Hospitality

Four hotels across PNG, plus meeting and event venues:

- **Airways Hotel** — Port Moresby (international standard, 4-star)
- **Gateway Hotel** — Port Moresby (3-star, business focused)
- **Highlander Hotel** — Mount Hagen (highlands business hub)
- **Huon Gulf Hotel** — Lae (industrial city)

Each hotel offers restaurant, bar, gym, pool, meeting rooms, and airport transfer.

### 4. Joint Ventures

Over 20 part-owned businesses, including:

- **Colgate Palmolive PNG** (50% owned) — FMCG distribution
- **Truk Stop PNG** (40% owned) — truck stop and fuel retail
- **Highlands Honey Cooperative** (30% owned) — honey export

We partner with leading PNG and international businesses to bring world-class products and services to the country.
