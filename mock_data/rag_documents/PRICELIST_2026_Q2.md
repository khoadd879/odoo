## 6. Discount authority

| Discount % | Approval required |

JV partner rates: negotiated annually with CFO sign-off (not on standard price list).

## 7. How to use this list in a quotation

1. Open the client CRM record.
2. Click "New Quotation" — the system suggests the standard price for the route.
3. If a contract price exists (different price list), Odoo applies that automatically.
4. Add a discount only within your approval authority. Above that, the system will block and ask for manager approval (per Sale Order Approval workflow).
