## 1. Purpose

Define how Less than Container Load (LCL) cargo is received, consolidated, and loaded at Steamships warehouses in Lae and Port Moresby.

## 2. Warehouse operating hours

- Lae: Monday to Friday 07:00 to 17:00, Saturday 07:00 to 12:00
- Port Moresby: Monday to Friday 07:30 to 17:30, Saturday closed
- Public holidays: closed unless pre-approved 48 hours in advance

## 3. Receiving LCL cargo

| Step | Action | Owner |
|------|--------|-------|
| 1 | Truck arrives at gate; driver presents Delivery Order | Gate Officer |
| 2 | Verify DO against booking in Odoo | Gate Officer |
| 3 | Weigh truck (gross / tare) on weighbridge | Weighbridge Operator |
| 4 | Unload at the assigned bay; photograph each piece | Warehouse Crew |
| 5 | Scan consignment barcode into Odoo (creates stock.move.line) | Warehouse Clerk |
| 6 | Move to consolidation zone for that vessel/voyage | Warehouse Crew |
| 7 | Issue Warehouse Receipt to client within 4 hours | Warehouse Clerk |

## 4. Consolidation rules

- LCL cargo is consolidated by destination port, then by vessel/voyage.
- Maximum stack height: 1.8 m (safety limit).
- Dangerous goods are stored in the dedicated DG cage (see SOP-SHIP-007).
- Reefer cargo is plugged in within 30 minutes of arrival.
- Fragile cargo is marked with a yellow tag and stored on the top layer.

## 5. Storage charges

Free storage: 3 working days from Warehouse Receipt date.

After that, daily storage charges per cubic metre:

| Cargo type | PGK per m³ per day |
|-----------|--------------------|
| General dry | 4.50 |
| Fragile / valuable | 9.00 |
| Reefer (includes power) | 15.00 |
| Dangerous goods | 12.00 |

## 6. Loading onto vessel

- Cargo is loaded onto the assigned vessel 24 hours before ETD.
- A "Loaded Manifest" is generated from Odoo and signed by the vessel chief officer.
- Any short-shipped cargo is recorded in Odoo and the client is notified by email within 2 hours.
