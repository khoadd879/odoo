# SOP-FIN-005 — Tenant Credit Check Procedure

**Document ID:** SOP-FIN-005
**Version:** 3.0
**Effective Date:** 2026-04-15
**Owner:** Credit Manager — Anne Tove
**Visibility:** STAFF ONLY

---

## 1. Purpose

Establish the credit assessment process for new tenants of Steamships Property. Prevents leasing to tenants with insufficient financial standing who could default on rent.

## 2. When credit check applies

- All new commercial leases (no exception for "well-known" brands)
- Lease renewals where the rent increases by more than 25%
- Assignment of lease to a new tenant
- Change of controlling shareholder of an existing tenant

## 3. Documents required

1. Two years of audited financial statements
2. Latest management accounts (within 90 days)
3. Bank reference letter (from the tenant's main operating bank)
4. Two trade references (from current suppliers)
5. Director guarantees for private companies
6. Latest tax compliance certificate (IRC for PNG-domiciled, equivalent for foreign)

## 4. Credit scoring

Each applicant is scored on:

| Factor | Weight | Pass threshold |
|--------|--------|----------------|
| Liquidity ratio (current assets / current liabilities) | 25% | >= 1.2 |
| Profitability (net margin, 2-year average) | 15% | >= 3% |
| Gearing (debt / equity) | 20% | <= 2.0 |
| Trade reference average rating | 20% | >= 3.5 of 5 |
| Years in operation | 10% | >= 3 years |
| Director credit history (PNG Credit Data Bureau) | 10% | No current defaults |

## 5. Decision matrix

| Score | Decision | Required approval |
|-------|----------|-------------------|
| 85-100 | Auto-approve | Leasing Manager |
| 70-84 | Approve with conditions (e.g. higher deposit) | Head of Property |
| 50-69 | Manual review by Credit Committee | Head of Property + CFO |
| Below 50 | Decline | Credit Manager notifies client |
